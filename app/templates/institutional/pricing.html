Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?

We bill annually based on your enrolled student count. You can adjust your license count at renewal time. For mid-year additions, we prorate the cost.

What's included in the per-user price?

Every user gets full access to all Klass Pro features, including AI-powered study tools, flashcards with spaced repetition, and unlimited notes. No feature tiers within the product.

Can we pilot Klass before committing?

Yes! We offer free 30-day pilots for departments or cohorts. This lets you evaluate the impact on student outcomes before an institution-wide rollout.

Do you offer academic discounts?

Our institutional pricing is already significantly discounted compared to individual subscriptions. Contact us for additional discounts for community colleges and public institutions.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PO/invoice for annual contracts, as well as credit card payments. NET-30 terms are available for qualifying institutions.
